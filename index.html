Product

Metrics That Matter

Evolving visual world makes many traditional metrics incomplete and even obsolete. Measuring key factors that affect your business is nonetheless more important than ever. New ways of generation and consumption of video require new tools to find and track your visual and brand metrics.

Context-aware engagement

Mass changes in habits of video generation and consumption require different models of engagement. Anything considered intrusive will be easily bypassed. Context is everything. Finding the right context to promote will generate maximum effectiveness and decide winners.

Data-driven decisions

Accurate measurement of visual data opens up new opportunities. Our analytics platform enables you to drill down on metrics that is important to you. Integration with non-visual data that you already rely upon is seamless - leverage visual data without overhauling your existing processes.

Strategy to Action

Performance data of your visual and brand assets enable you to better plan and position. Customized visual metrics empower you to handle shifting markets and adapt to competitive landscape. Be it actionable adjustment, acountability or ROI boost, now you have tools to take action.

Technology

Our platform is powered by visual machine learning, a branch of Artificial Intelligence (AI) that is rapidly going mainstream. We have invested significantly in building a resilient distributed infrastructure that scales with number of concurrent streams.

Our unique approach efficiently processes large sets of data to serve powerful analytics through Cloud based API. Our customers can slice-and-dice such data in intuitive applications. Most importantly, we have built the entire platform with end users in mind.
