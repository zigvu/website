About

We believe this can revolutionize the way many media verticals currently function.

Track What Matters
Evolving visual world makes many traditional metrics incomplete and even obsolete. Measuring key factors that affect your business is nonetheless more important than ever. New ways of generation and consumption of video requires new tools to find and track your visual and brand assets.

Contextualize
Changing video viewing habits and devices require different models of engagement. Anything considered intrusive will be easily bypassed. Context is everything. Finding the right context to promote will generate maximum effectiveness and decide winners.

Strategy & Action
Visual performance data of your content enable you to better plan and position. Customized metrics allow you to handle shifting markets and adapt to competitive landscape. Be it actionable adjustment or ROI boost, now you have tools to do that.

Measure & Monitor
Accurate measurement of visual content opens up new opportunities. Our powerful analytics enable drilling down on important metrics. When used alongside non-visual data, it enables more sophisticated analysis. Close monitoring of visibility drives accountability and improves effectiveness.

Technology

Our platform is powered by visual machine learning, a branch of Artificial Intelligence (AI) that is rapidly going mainstream. It is data heavy and we have invented new ways to make that efficient. The platform scales smoothly with visual streams to derive measurable metrics.

Our unique approach allows us to provide accurate profiling of visual content. It is driven by Cloud based API framework that leverages state-of-the-art computing infrastructure. Our customers can slice-and-dice these metrics to track running performance as well as generate powerful analytics. We are pushing boundaries on what is possible with such technologies.
