About

Metrics That Matter

In a world that is increasingly visual, analytics required to make critical business decisions needs to go beyond simply aggregating titles, links or text tags. While these traditional metrics have become obsolete, measuring key factors that affect your business have nonetheless become more important than ever.

Context-aware Engagement

Changing video consumption habits necessitates new models of consumer engagement. Anything considered intrusive will be easily bypassed. Context is everything. Finding the right context to promote will generate maximum effectiveness and decide winning strategies.

Data-driven Decisions

Accurate measurement of visual content opens up new opportunities. Our powerful analytics platform enables you to drill down on metrics that is important to you. Integration with non-visual data that you already rely upon is easy and seamless - enabling you to leverage visual data without overhauling your business processes.

Strategy to Action

With our tools to find and track your visual and brand assets, the path from strategy to action will be shorter. With metrics customized to your strategy needs, you will be able to react to shifting market conditions or respond to competitive actions not in a matter of months but days.
